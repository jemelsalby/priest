Notifications

VISION

To impart education to all in a healthy environment to build a strong nation of responsible citizens and to promote literacy and craftsmanship in our country. To foster a spirit of unity and to shape a nation of enlightened and responsible citizens through such educational institutions.

MISSION

To plan, provide, and coordinate the provision of services in the cause of education, in particular to its member constituents, especially in the matter of the sharing of personnel, solving problems with curricula, and valuing education.

OBJECTIVES

To advise and guide member constituents and other educational institutions and educators as they seek assistance on all matters related to education, whether administrative, legal, financial, or otherwise. To constitute, offer, and grant prizes, awards, scholarships, and stipends in furtherance of the objects of the CSA and to take over the administration of scholarships and prizes.
